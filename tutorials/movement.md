
# Move Your Sprite
In this tutoirial we will look at creating a sprite and making it move. 
## Step 1: Create a sprite
Add the block:
||sprites:create sprite of kind Player||

## Step 2: Add movement
Add:
||controller:move mySprite with buttons||

## Step 3: Test your game
Click ▶️ to run your game.

## Congratulations!
You can now control your character!
